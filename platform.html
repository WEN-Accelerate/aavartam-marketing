The Platform

Real screens.
Real strategic work.

Everything below is the actual product — the workspace where strategy, operations and projects get structured, delivered and watched. No mockups, no concept art.

Portfolio Command Centre

Every project. One screen. Live.

The Monday-morning view every leader wants: all projects organised by health — On Track, Watch, At Risk, Delivered — with partner, office and progress against plan on every card. The work that needs attention surfaces itself.

  • Health-first board — projects grouped by live status, derived from real progress, not self-reported optimism
  • Filters that match how you think — by office, partner, stage and health, with instant search
  • Same-day onboarding — import your current project list by CSV and the board is live
Project Workspace

A structured home for every engagement

Each project carries its financial profile, type, health and active module on one screen. Revenue, growth and EBITDA sit beside the state of the work — because strategic decisions need both at once.

  • Typed projects — Growth Strategy, Operations Improvement, M&A Due Diligence, Digital & AI Transformation and more
  • Financial profile built in — multi-year revenue, growth and EBITDA with margins calculated for you
  • Active module focus — the module in flight, its research questions, resources and desired output, front and centre
Modules, Questions & Findings

Hypothesis-driven delivery, built into the workspace

Projects decompose into modules; modules decompose into research questions; questions get answered with findings. It's the discipline of a top-tier engagement team, enforced gently by the structure of the tool.

  • Workplans in one click — proven module structures generated per project type, editable to fit
  • Findings, not vibes — each question carries its research approach, status and evidence
  • AI-generated questions — agents propose the research questions a module needs, you keep editorial control
AI Workbench & Knowledge Base

AI that knows your engagement, not just the internet

Proposals, steering-committee minutes, client transcripts, primary and secondary research — indexed into the engagement's knowledge base. Ask anything; the answer is grounded in your sources. Studio actions run the heavyweight work.

  • Source-grounded chat — select the sources, ask the question, get an answer anchored in your own documents
  • Studio actions — hypothesis trees, interview plans, synthesis per question, SCQA steering-committee reports, competitor tracking
  • One library, every output — upload a document once and every agent works from it
Agile Project Planning

Strategy, run with the discipline of a sprint

The planner turns modules and research questions into a working agile board — tasks with owners, priorities, due dates and tags, moving through Not Started, In Progress, In Review and Completed.

  • Import from modules — research questions become tasks in one click, traced back to the strategy they advance
  • AI task generation — two weeks of practical work proposed from the project's current state
  • A rhythm leaders can see — completion rolls up to project health on the portfolio board
