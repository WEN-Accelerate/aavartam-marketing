The Problem

Everyone has a strategy.
Almost no one can see it running.

Strategic work lives in decks, inboxes and people's heads. Whoever you are — a firm running client engagements or a leadership team running your own initiatives — the failure mode is the same: no structure, no live visibility, and expensive hours spent on work machines should do.

Consulting Firms

Partners find out last

Engagement status arrives when someone remembers to send it. Without a live view across clients, modules and deliverables, risks compound quietly until a client escalates — and renewals pay the price.

Family Offices

Holdings report in, nobody rolls up

Each operating company and investment initiative reports differently, on its own cycle, in its own format. The principals see polished quarterly decks — never the live state of the strategic agenda across the portfolio.

High-Growth SMBs

Strategy can't afford a strategy team

The annual plan is written with conviction and then run through spreadsheets and standing meetings. Big-firm consulting rigour costs more than the initiatives themselves — so execution runs on instinct.

Independent Consultants

One person, five clients, zero leverage

Research, decks, notes, trackers, follow-ups — the delivery machinery of a firm, run by one person at midnight. Quality holds, but capacity caps your practice and prep eats the hours clients actually pay for.

Why Aavartam

One platform.
Three kinds of leverage.

Software alone doesn't execute strategy, and neither do tools bolted together. Aavartam combines structure, machine intelligence and human expertise — in proportion to what each piece of work actually needs.

How It Works

From ambition to evidence,
one operating rhythm

1

Structure the work

Pick a project type — Growth Strategy, Operations Improvement, M&A Due Diligence and more. Aavartam scaffolds the modules and research questions, and ingests your documents, transcripts and financials into a structured knowledge base.

2

Deliver with leverage

Put agents on the research and analysis. Plan execution on the agile board. Run meetings through the meeting platform. Pull in a human expert from the network when a question needs lived experience.

3

See everything, live

Findings roll up to modules; modules roll up to project health; projects roll up to a portfolio view. Whoever needs the picture — partner, principal or founder — gets it live, not at the quarterly review.

AI Workbench & Knowledge Base

Strategic Modules & Findings

Strategy broken into questions you can answer

Each project decomposes into modules, and each module into research questions with findings — the same hypothesis-driven discipline top firms use, built into the workspace. Progress means evidence, not activity.

  • Typed workplans — proven module structures per project type, generated in one click
  • Question-level tracking — every research question carries its approach, status and finding
  • Findings roll up — module status and project health derive from real progress
Agile Project Planning

From research questions to a working board

Import tasks straight from your modules or let the planner generate two weeks of practical work — interviews to schedule, analyses to run, documents to draft. Then run it like any high-performing team: priorities, owners, due dates, done.

  • One click from plan to board — tasks generated from modules and research questions
  • Full agile flow — not started, in progress, in review, completed, with owners and priorities
  • Tied to the strategy — every task traces back to the module it advances
