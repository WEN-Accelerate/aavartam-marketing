The Problem

By the time the quarterly review
flags it, it's already too late

Engagement status lives in people's heads, last month's decks and a dozen spreadsheets. Partners and practice leads are the last to know when delivery drifts — and the cost of finding out late is measured in renewals.

No live read on engagement health

Status updates arrive when someone remembers to send them. Without a single live view across engagements, modules and deliverables, risks compound quietly until a client escalates.

Knowledge scattered across silos

Call transcripts in inboxes, strategy docs in Drive, financials in spreadsheets. Every new engagement — and every new team member — starts from zero instead of from everything the firm already knows.

Prep eats the hours clients pay for

Consultants spend more than half their time on research, decks, notes and status reporting — not the judgement and insight clients actually hire them for. That ratio needs to flip.

Delivery structure is reinvented every time

Workplans, deliverable lists and trackers get rebuilt engagement by engagement. Without a shared delivery framework, quality depends entirely on who happens to be staffed.

How It Works

From kickoff to renewal,
one operating rhythm

Three steps from a signed engagement to a portfolio you can see at a glance.

1

Structure the engagement

Pick from eight engagement types — Growth Strategy to M&A Due Diligence. Aavartam scaffolds the modules, deliverables and assessment dimensions automatically, and AI ingests your transcripts, briefs and financials into a structured knowledge base.

2

Deliver with AI leverage

Run AI research in the Workbench, generate consulting-grade slide decks from live engagement data, and hold voice or video AI consultant sessions — every output grounded in that client's own knowledge base.

3

See everything, live

Deliverable progress rolls up to module health; module health rolls up to engagement health; engagement health rolls up to your portfolio view. Weekly AI-summarised check-ins keep it honest without chasing anyone.
