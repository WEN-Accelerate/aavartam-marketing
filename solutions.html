For Corporate Strategy & PE Teams

Run internal initiatives like engagements

Strategy teams and PE portfolio operators juggle initiatives across business units or portfolio companies — usually in spreadsheets and quarterly decks. Aavartam treats each one as a structured engagement with live health, so leadership sees reality between reviews, not just at them.

  • Portfolio-wide health — every initiative or portfolio company tracked on one screen, with financial profiles alongside delivery status
  • Structured value-creation plans — modules and deliverables per initiative, from operations improvement to digital transformation and M&A diligence
  • Board-ready in minutes — investment committee and leadership updates generated from live data, not assembled the night before
  • Less reliance on external firms — in-house teams deliver consulting-grade structure and research with AI leverage
